Hadi's Code of Ethics

I aim to live a life guided by excellence, creativity, growth, collaboration, honesty, compassion, and respect for others, aligning my actions with these values and letting them inform my decision-making in all aspects of my life.

My Driving Values

Moral Values

  • I strive to be true to myself and act in a way that is genuine and authentic.

  • I believe in showing kindness and empathy towards others, and treating them with dignity and respect.

Personal Values

  • I strive to develop not only my technical abilities, but also my interpersonal skills such as effective communication, collaboration, and leadership.

  • I value creativity as an essential tool for problem-solving and innovation, always seeking to bring fresh perspectives and think outside the box.

Professional Values

  • I aspire to embody empathy and effective communication, committing myself to the tasks at hand and recognizing their significance to others.

  • I value building strong relationships with colleagues and stakeholders, and believe in the power of teamwork to achieve success and personal development.

I believe that by being compassionate, promoting growth, striving for excellence, fostering creativity, and maintaining honesty, I can collaborate with my stakeholders to establish meaningful and enduring relationships.

Salient Stakeholder Groups

  • The trust of my family and friends is invaluable to me, as they provide me with emotional support and connection during challenging times. When I was feeling stressed from work and school, my friends organized a fun laser tag party as a wonderful display of their support and care for my well-being, and we took a picture together to commemorate the moment.

  • My commitment to excellence extends beyond being a reliable and productive member of my employer; I also prioritize treating colleagues with respect and dignity, and collaborating with them to foster their growth and development. As a compassionate and empathetic person, I take pride in coaching and supporting colleagues, particularly new hires, to build their confidence and become more effective contributors, which has also helped me grow as a potential leader.

  • I believe in the importance of giving back to the community through volunteer work and charitable donations. Whether it's distributing presents to children at the DMC hospital or donating to pancreatic cancer causes in honor of my late father, I strive to make a positive impact and contribute to meaningful causes.

  • As a firm believer in environmental responsibility and preserving our planet for future generations, I actively participate in initiatives that promote sustainability. One such instance was my volunteer work for the "Greening of Detroit" initiative a few years ago where we planted 1000 trees, and I can't wait for those trees to grow!

As highlighted in my Personal Ethical Dilemma Paper, I recognize that I made a mistake by solely focusing on my colleague's situation and not considering the impact on other stakeholders associated with my employer. Now, I am committed to taking a step back when faced with conflicting values or stakeholder interests, analyzing the situation from multiple perspectives, identifying the root cause of the conflict, and making a fair decision that aligns with my values and benefits all parties involved.

Ten years from now, I hope to be an ethical person who continues to prioritize compassion, growth and honesty in all areas of my life. I believe that the universal ethical principles of honesty, integrity, respect, and empathy can guide me no matter what dilemma I face.

Ultimately, my goal is to live a life that is fulfilling and meaningful, while making a positive impact on the world around me. I hope to achieve this by embodying my core values, building strong relationships with my stakeholders, and making decisions that are aligned with my ethical beliefs.

Moving forward, I recognize the need for changes in my approach to building and maintaining relationships with my important stakeholders. This involves prioritizing open communication, active listening, conflict resolution, and aligning my actions with my code of ethics. I plan to seek out diverse perspectives and consult trusted individuals to foster creativity in my decision-making process, and to hold myself accountable for upholding my values.
